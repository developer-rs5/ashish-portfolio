About Me

From Sikar, Rajasthan to the Cricket Field

My name is Ashish Sharma, and I hail from the historic city of Sikar in Rajasthan. Cricket has been my passion since childhood, and I've dedicated my life to mastering the sport, particularly in the format of tennis cricket.

Through years of rigorous training, discipline, and competitive play, I've risen to become the #1 tennis cricket player in Sikar and have earned recognition as one of the top cricketers across Rajasthan.

My journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Every match is an opportunity to push boundaries and set new standards in the sport I love.
